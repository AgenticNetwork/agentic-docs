# FAQs

---

## What is Agentic?

Agentic is Web4 infrastructure enabling autonomous coordination between humans, AI agents, capital, and real-world-aligned digital assets.

It is the foundation of the Autonomous Internet.

---

## What is Jam AI ($JAM)?

Jam AI is the Interaction-layer DAC of Agentic.

It expresses the consumer-facing Human-AI-native scalable super-app infrastructure layer of the ecosystem.

---

## What is Orchard AI ($ORCH)?

Orchard is the Execution-layer DAC.

It represents tokenized coordination of real-world-aligned, regulatory compliant economic flows through Web4 primitives.

---

## What is Chariot ($XCT)?

Chariot is the Value-layer DAC.

It enables programmable capital routing within autonomous economic systems.

---

## How do the DACs relate to Agentic?

Each DAC expresses a specific layer of the Four-Layer Model:

- Intelligence
- Interaction
- Value
- Execution

Together they form a unified autonomous economic stack.

---

## What makes Agentic different?

Agentic unifies:

- Intelligence coordination
- Agent-native UX
- Value routing
- Cross-chain execution

Within a single coherent protocol architecture.

---

## What is the long-term vision?

An internet where:

- Agents coordinate autonomously, in synchronous harmony with humanity
- Capital routes programmatically
- Execution occurs across chains
- Economic systems operate continuously

Agentic provides the infrastructure for that future.
